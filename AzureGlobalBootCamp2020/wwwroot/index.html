Global Azure Virtual 2020 UK & Ireland

All around the world user groups and communities want to learn about Azure and Cloud Computing!
We are organising an event and looking for people all over the Globe to take part.

We are looking for contributions on any Azure Topic, don't be put off if someone is already doing that topic.

Your take on the subject will be different, and we encourage all contributions.
Note: In case you want to contribute with a Live session, please contact Allen O'Neill .

What we provide

  • A dedicated Youtube Channel to host your video contribution(s).
  • Support to create pre-recorded or Live stream content.

Our Ask

  • If you haven't already please fill out this form.
  • Join the CloudFamily Slack Channel if you haven't already.
  • A video contribution, let's say 15-45 min video on an Azure topic.
  • We will host this and supply a OneDrive link for you to upload too, if needed.
  • Any social media posts please include the hashtag #GlobalAzureVirtual.

Why Join Us

  • An opportunity to take part in a virtual Azure community event
  • Learn from the best in the industry
  • Discover the best tools and practices within Azure
  • Share content with developers from all over the world
  • Grow your network

Speaker Name

Andy Morrell

Senior Software Devleoper

Andy has been a developer since a young age, and professionally for the last 16 years. After his uncle (a developer) turned up in a TVR Griffith at 8 years old, the same year he got a ZX Spectrum for Christmas, that was almost a career choice made. When Andy finished formal education he went on to use the Microsoft .Net stack during the majority of his career. In recent years has developed skills in Cloud Architecture and building software in service providers, such as AWS and Azure. Andy was recently given a Microsoft MVP (Most Valuable Professional) Award in Developer Technologies for 2020/21, in recognition for his work in the development community.
